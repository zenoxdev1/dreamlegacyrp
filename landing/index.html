Access flow

How to Join

The steps below keep the community organized and make sure each player is ready before joining.

Requirements

  1. PlayStation 3 console.
  2. Custom Firmware (CFW) or HEN installed.
  3. A valid Discord account.
  4. GTAO: Reloaded installed.
  5. A new PSN account for roleplay.
  6. A new GTA Online character.

Enlistment

  1. Join the Dream Legacy RP, and RLO Discord servers.
  2. Complete the required verification.
  3. Submit a whitelist application.
  4. Once approved, you are ready to connect.
